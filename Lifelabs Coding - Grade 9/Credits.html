About Us

"Enhance Your Play"

_________________

Hello! We are GameXplain; a small channel that aims to teach the average gamer how to improve
their gameplay by sharing tactics, techniques, and various tips and tricks

Skill Of Choice

Our chosen skill of choice is Critical Thinking. We believe that this is the skill that is not only the
most used in gaming, but the skill that can also be improved by watching and learning from gaming content.

Content Type

We chose gaming content as it is one of the most popular and relevant content types that can be seen on YouTube.

Brand Name

Our brand name is GameXplain. The meaning behind it is already in its name. Our content is aimed
towards helping gamers learn different strategies, tactics, and tips and tricks in the various games that
they play. To put it simply, we look to explain multiple aspects of common online video games.
